# Privacy Policy for Glimpse

*Last updated: August 7, 2025*

## Data Collection and Usage

### What Data We Collect
- **API Keys**: Stored locally in your browser for connecting to AI services (Gemini, OpenRouter)
- **Chat History**: Stored locally in your browser for session management
- **File Uploads**: Processed locally and sent to AI services you configure
- **Screenshots**: Captured locally and sent to AI services you configure

### How We Use Your Data
- **AI Processing**: Your messages, files, and screenshots are sent to the AI service you choose (Gemini or OpenRouter) for processing
- **Local Storage**: Chat history and settings are stored in your browser's local storage
- **YouTube Transcripts**: When analyzing YouTube videos, transcripts may be fetched from our optional backend service

### Data Sharing
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not share your data with third parties except for the AI services you explicitly configure
- **AI Service Providers**: Your data is sent to Gemini (Google) or OpenRouter based on your settings
- **No Analytics**: We do not collect analytics or usage data

### Data Storage
- **Local Only**: All settings, API keys, and chat history are stored locally in your browser
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your data on our servers
- **Optional Backend**: YouTube transcript service (if used) does not store transcripts

### Your Rights
- **Data Control**: You can clear all data by removing the extension or clearing browser data
- **API Key Management**: You can change or remove API keys at any time through settings
- **Chat History**: You can delete chat history through the extension interface

### Security
- **Local Storage**: API keys and data are stored using browser's secure storage mechanisms
- **HTTPS**: All API communications use encrypted HTTPS connections
- **No Data Transmission**: No data is sent to our servers except for optional YouTube transcript service

### Third-Party Services
When you use this extension, your data may be sent to:
- **Google Gemini AI**: Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **OpenRouter**: Subject to [OpenRouter's Privacy Policy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy)
- **YouTube Transcript Service**: Optional backend service for transcript extraction

### Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be reflected in the extension updates and on our website.

For privacy concerns or questions, please contact us at: aziz@briskl.tech
